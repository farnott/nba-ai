Questions

Straightforward answers.

Does it replace my phone?

No. Calls are simply forwarded when you want Mandy to answer. You stay in control.

Can I still answer calls myself?

Absolutely. If you're free, answer as normal. Mandy only steps in when you can't.

I already have voicemail. Why is this different?

Voicemail takes messages… if people bother to leave one. Mandy answers the call immediately, has a natural conversation, gathers the information you need, and makes sure potential customers don't slip away.

How do I get the details from the call?

As soon as the call ends, you'll receive the caller's details and a summary of what they needed. No listening to voicemails or scribbling down messages… just the important information, ready when you are.

Do I need a computer?

No. If you can use a mobile phone, you're all set. Most customers simply receive the enquiry details and carry on with their day.

My spouse answers the phone when I can't. Why would I need this?

Lots of businesses rely on family members to help out, and that's absolutely fine. Mandy simply makes sure every call is answered consistently… even when everyone's busy, out shopping, enjoying dinner, or taking a well-earned break.

Do I have to set it up myself?

No. We handle the entire setup for you and make sure everything is working properly before you start using it.

Do I need any software?

No software to install and nothing complicated to learn. Mandy works with your existing phone number.

How long does setup take?

Usually within one business day. We'll configure everything for you and send your dedicated AI number as soon as your account is ready.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime before your next billing date. No contracts, no awkward conversations, and no questions asked.
